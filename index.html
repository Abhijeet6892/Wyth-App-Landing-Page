How WYTH works

Six mechanics. Zero compromise. Built to actually find your person.

3 Slots. Not 3000.
You get 3 connection slots. A slot only returns when a connection closes — accepted, declined, or expired after 7 days. Scarcity is permanent. Every choice matters.
🎯
Declare your intent
Exploring, Dating, or Ready for Marriage. Your feed shows only people who carry the same intent. No confusion. No wasted time.
🤝
Vouched by real people
People in your network vouch for your character. No catfishing. No fake profiles. Every person is someone a real person trusts.

Ghosting is impossible
Connections have a natural lifecycle. Nudges, closure prompts, and a culture of acknowledgement mean every interaction ends with respect — even if it's just one line.

AI that helps you connect
Personalised conversation starters, bio help, closure drafts. WYTH helps you say what you actually mean.

Milestone-guided relationships
First message → Call → Date → Family → Commitment. Clear steps. No ambiguity. Just progress.
